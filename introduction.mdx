## Overview

AccordRx is an online marketplace built specifically for pharmacies to buy and sell overstock medications. You save time and money with a flat-rate pricing model while staying compliant with DSCSA requirements. Focus on serving patients by easily managing inventory without the hassle of destruction fees or supply chain disruptions.

Our platform offers a secure, user-friendly experience with over 12 years of pharmacy expertise backing it. Whether you need to offload excess stock or source hard-to-find prescriptions, AccordRx puts pharmacies first.

## Flat-Rate Pricing Model

Enjoy predictable costs with AccordRx's simple subscription. You access unlimited listings, searches, and transactions for a fixed monthly fee. This model saves thousands compared to platforms charging per sale or high restocking penalties.

<Callout kind="success">
  Switch to flat-rate and redirect savings toward patient care or business growth.

## DSCSA Compliance and Security

AccordRx handles DSCSA requirements seamlessly. You upload serialized data once, and our system generates compliant reports for transfers. Built with top-tier security, your transactions remain protected.
