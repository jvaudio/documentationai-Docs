## Overview

<Callout kind="info">
Manage your AccordRx account settings through the dashboard to keep your pharmacy information current, customize notifications, and handle billing efficiently. These configurations ensure smooth operations for buying and selling overstock medications.
</Callout>

Access your account settings from the dashboard at `https://app.accordrx.com`. Navigate to the user menu (top-right corner) and select `Account Settings`.

## Updating Your Pharmacy Profile

Keep your pharmacy profile accurate to avoid delays in transactions and compliance checks.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Sign in to `https://app.accordrx.com` using your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Profile" icon="user">
    Click your profile icon and select `Edit Profile`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update Details">
    Enter your pharmacy name, address, NPI number, and contact email/phone.
    
    Example fields:
    
    | Field          | Example Value                  |
    |----------------|--------------------------------|
    | Pharmacy Name  | Madison Family Pharmacy        |
    | Address        | 123 Main St, Madison, WI 53703 |
    | NPI            | 1234567890                     |
    | Phone          | (608) 555-0123                 |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes" icon="save">
    Review and click `Update Profile`. Confirm via email if prompted.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Verify your NPI and address match official records to comply with DSCSA requirements.
</Callout>

## Configuring Preferences

Customize your account to match your workflow.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Payment & Billing" icon="credit-card">

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Add Payment Method">
        Go to `Billing` tab and select `Add Card`.
        
````html
Credit Card: **** **** **** 1234
Expires: 12/2026
CVV: ***
````
        
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set Billing Preferences">
        Choose auto-pay or manual invoicing.
        
        Available options:
        
        | Preference     | Description                          |
        |----------------|--------------------------------------|
        | Auto-Pay       | Charges flat-rate fee monthly        |
        | Manual         | Receive invoice via email            |
        | Currency       | USD (default)                        |
      </Step>
    </Steps>

  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Notifications" icon="bell">

    Manage alerts for listings, sales, and low stock.

    <Expandable title="Email Preferences" default-open="true">
      - Transaction confirmations: Enabled by default
      - Price alerts: Set thresholds (e.g., `<{threshold}` for buys)
      - Weekly summaries: Opt-in
      
      Update via `Notifications > Email`.
    </Expandable>

    <Expandable title="In-App Alerts">
      - Push notifications for mobile app
      - Browser alerts for dashboard activity
    </Expandable>

  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Accessing Transaction History

Review your buy/sell activity for reporting and audits.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open History" icon="history">
    From dashboard, select `Transactions`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter Records">
    Use filters:
    
    | Filter Type    | Options                     |
    |----------------|-----------------------------|
    | Date Range     | Last 30 days, Custom        |
    | Type           | Buy, Sell, Transfer         |
    | Status         | Completed, Pending, Failed  |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Data">
    Download CSV for accounting software.
  </Step>
</Steps>
