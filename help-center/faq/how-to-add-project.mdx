## Overview

To add a new project in Accordrx, simply use the dashboard.

## Steps

1. Log in to your Accordrx account.
2. Click on "Projects" in the sidebar.
3. Select "Add Project".
4. Enter your project's name and details.
5. Click "Create" to finish.

You can now start organizing your documentation under the new project.
