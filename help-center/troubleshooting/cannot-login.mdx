---
title: Cannot Log In
description: Quick fix for Accordrx login issues.
---

## Symptoms

- Login fails or shows error messages
- Forgot password doesn't work

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Reset Password">
    ````
    Use the 'Forgot Password' button on the login screen.
    Check your email for reset instructions.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Account Status">
    ````
    Make sure your account is activated and email verified.
    Contact support if you still can't log in.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
