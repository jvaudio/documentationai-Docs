description: How to structure documentation in Accordrx.
---

## Overview

Use folders and pages to keep your documentation tidy.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a Folder">
    ````
    In your project, click "New Folder".
    Name your folder (e.g., "API Docs").
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Pages">
    ````
    Open the folder.
    Click "Add Page" and give it a clear title.
    Repeat for each section of your docs.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
