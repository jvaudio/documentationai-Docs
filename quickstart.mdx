## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A valid pharmacy license and NPI number
- A business email address (e.g., `pharmacy@yourdomain.com`)
- Access to your pharmacy's DEA registration details
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Safari)
</Callout>

## Create Your Account

Follow these steps to register for AccordRx.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Registration Page" icon="external-link">
    Navigate to [https://accordrx.com/registration](https://accordrx.com/registration) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Your Details" icon="user">
    Provide your pharmacy name, email, phone number, and create a strong password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Agree to Terms" icon="file-text">
    Review and accept the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit Registration" icon="check-circle">
    Click **Create Account**. You'll receive a verification email.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Log In and Explore the Dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for the verification link from `support@accordrx.com` and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the login page, enter your credentials, and access the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Dashboard Tour" icon="activity">
    Review key sections: Inventory, Listings, Orders, and Reports.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Verify Your Pharmacy Details

Pharmacy verification ensures compliance with DSCSA requirements.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Profile" icon="settings">
    Click your profile icon in the top-right and select **Pharmacy Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload Documents" icon="upload">
    Provide your NPI, DEA number, license scan, and proof of address.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit for Review" icon="send">
    Submit for approval. Approval typically takes 24-48 hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="List Overstock (Seller)" icon="package">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to Listings" icon="plus">
        From the dashboard, select **New Listing**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enter Medication Details" icon="edit-3">
        Add drug name (e.g., "Lisinopril 10mg"), quantity (e.g., 100), expiration date, and lot number.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set Price and Publish" icon="dollar-sign">
        Use flat-rate pricing and click **Publish**. Your listing is now live!
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Search for Stock (Buyer)" icon="search">
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Access Marketplace" icon="shopping-bag">
        Click **Marketplace** on the dashboard.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Filter Results" icon="filter">
        Search by drug name, dosage, or expiration date.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Purchase" icon="shopping-cart">
        Select a listing, review details, and complete the order.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip" title="Pro Tip">
Monitor your email for order notifications and approvals. Use the dashboard's Reports section to track savings from overstock transactions.
</Callout>
